Five Point Someone

Author:Chetan Bhagat

About:Five Point Someone: What not to do at IIT is a 2004 novel written by Indian author Chetan Bhagat. The book has sold over a million copies worldwide. The films 3 Idiots and Nanban are based on the book. It was also adapted into a play by the theatre company Evam.
